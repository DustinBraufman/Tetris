The main goal of Tetris is to arrange the falling blocks into lines in order to prevent the tetrominoes from stacking above the top border. The player can use their controls to rotate and move the block as it is falling so that they stack in a complimentary way. Once a horizontal row is completely full of blocks it will disappear and points will be awarded.

As the game progresses the speed gradually increases making it more difficult to stack the blocks in a beneficial manner. There are seven different types of blocks, all of which can fall at any given time.

Typically, there is a control to drop a block instantly to make the game progress faster, along with another control to hold a piece and drop it in turn for a different piece later.
