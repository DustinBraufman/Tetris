Tetris is a popular puzzle game created in 1984 by Russian computer scientist Alexey Pajitnov. Since the game's creation it has soared to become an international sensation having been played by at least a billion people.

Tetris was inspired by the board game Pentominos and features colored blocks that are arranged into a space like its predecessor. The game gained popularity in the United States when video game designer Henk Rogers discovered the game and released it on the pc. Rogers then licensed the handheld rights to Tetris to Nintendo, it was then released on the gameboy and sold 35 million copies.

Since its inception there have been countless different variations of Tetris created for devices from computers to e-cigarettes. Tetris has become a cultural phenomenon and was even the first game in space. For more information on any aspect of the game click on the bar.
